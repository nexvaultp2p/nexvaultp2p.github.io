Plain Language Summary: NexVault collects only what we need to run a safe, compliant exchange service. We do not sell your data. We use it to verify your identity, process transactions, and comply with financial regulations in India and Nepal. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information when you register, complete KYC verification, conduct transactions, or contact our support team. The categories of data we collect are as follows:

Category Examples Purpose
Identity Data Full name, date of birth, nationality Account creation, KYC compliance
Contact Data Email address, phone number Account access, transaction alerts
Document Data Passport, national ID, driver's license, utility bills, bank statements KYC/AML regulatory compliance
Financial Data Transaction amounts, payment methods, wallet addresses, account numbers Processing transactions, fraud prevention
Transaction Proof Payment screenshots uploaded by users Manual transaction verification
Technical Data IP address, browser type, device ID, OS Security, fraud detection
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, session timestamps Service improvement, analytics
Communications Support emails, chat logs Customer support, dispute resolution

We do not collect sensitive data such as racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, or biometric data beyond what is embedded in submitted identity documents.

02

How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used strictly for the purposes described below. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties for marketing purposes.

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your NexVault account, authenticating logins, and enabling profile settings.
  • KYC & Identity Verification: Reviewing submitted documents to verify your identity and place of residence in compliance with applicable regulations.
  • Transaction Processing: Facilitating buy/sell orders, verifying payment screenshots, and disbursing funds or digital assets to your designated account.
  • AML & Fraud Prevention: Screening transactions for suspicious activity, maintaining risk profiles, and reporting to authorities as required by law.
  • Communications: Sending transaction confirmations, security alerts, policy updates, and responding to support queries.
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting obligations under Indian (PMLA, FEMA) and Nepali (BAFIA, AML Act) financial laws.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve platform usability, reliability, and features.
  • Dispute Resolution: Using transaction records and communications to investigate and resolve disputes.
03

Legal Basis for Processing

NexVault processes your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to provide the services you requested — account creation, transaction processing, and KYC verification.
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with AML/KYC regulations, tax reporting requirements, and law enforcement requests under Indian and Nepali law.
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud detection, security monitoring, and service improvement — where these interests do not override your fundamental rights.
  • Consent: Where we rely on consent (such as marketing communications), you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

For Indian users, our processing is governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the DPDP Act, 2023. For Nepali users, we follow the Individual Privacy Act, 2018 and guidelines from Nepal Rastra Bank.

04

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with KYC verification, cloud hosting, email delivery, or customer support — bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Payment Partners: When processing transactions through eSewa, Khalti, FonePay, PhonePe, or UPI, necessary payment details are shared with those processors.
  • Regulatory Authorities: Financial intelligence units, tax authorities, or law enforcement when required by law or a valid legal order in India or Nepal.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred as part of the transaction — with prior notice to users.
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing will only occur with your explicit consent.

All third-party service providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than delivering the agreed service to NexVault.

06

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law:

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 5 years after closure, in compliance with Indian PMLA requirements and Nepali AML obligations.
  • KYC Documents: Retained for 5 years from the date of submission or last transaction, as mandated by financial regulators.
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years to meet tax and anti-money laundering reporting requirements.
  • Payment Screenshots: Retained for 2 years for dispute resolution purposes, then securely deleted.
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3 years to assist with ongoing or future disputes.
  • Technical Logs: IP and access logs retained for 12 months for security purposes.

When retention periods expire, data is securely and permanently deleted or anonymized so it can no longer be linked to you personally.

08

Data Security

NexVault implements technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration:

  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Sensitive data at rest (documents, financial data) is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Access Controls: Internal access to user data is restricted on a strict need-to-know basis with role-based permissions and audit logging.
  • Infrastructure Security: Our servers are hosted with reputable cloud providers with SOC 2 Type II certification and physical security controls.
  • KYC Document Handling: Identity documents are stored in isolated, encrypted storage with strictly limited access, separate from other user data.
  • Breach Response: In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours of discovery.
  • Employee Training: All staff with access to user data receive mandatory data privacy and security training.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is completely immune to risk. If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@nexvault.io.

09

Cross-Border Data Transfers

NexVault operates across India and Nepal. Your data may be processed or stored in either country or by cloud infrastructure providers operating in compliant jurisdictions.

  • Any transfer of Indian user data outside India complies with the DPDP Act, 2023 requirements and applicable RBI guidelines.
  • Nepali user data is handled in accordance with directives from Nepal Rastra Bank and the Individual Privacy Act, 2018.
  • We ensure that any cross-border transfer of data is protected by appropriate safeguards, including contractual clauses binding the recipient to equivalent data protection standards.
  • Financial transaction records that must be retained for regulatory purposes will always be stored in accordance with the regulations of the user's home jurisdiction.
