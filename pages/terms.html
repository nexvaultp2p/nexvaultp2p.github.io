2. Description of Services

NexVault provides a manual peer-to-peer exchange platform facilitating the exchange of:

  • US Dollars (USD) against Indian Rupees (INR) and Nepalese Rupees (NPR)
  • Cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, USDT) against INR and NPR
  • Preloaded virtual Visa cards denominated in USD

All transactions are manually processed and verified by our team. NexVault does not operate as a bank, financial institution, or regulated money services business. This is a peer-to-peer facilitation service.
